About Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity involves protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks. With threats evolving every day, safeguarding sensitive information is more critical than ever.

Why Cybersecurity is Important

Cyber threats such as malware, phishing, and ransomware can compromise personal and organizational data. Effective cybersecurity measures help protect privacy, secure systems, and build trust.

Cybersecurity Best Practices

  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Enable multi-factor authentication
  • Regularly update software and systems
  • Educate your team about phishing scams
